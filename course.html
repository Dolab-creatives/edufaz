MBBS (MEDICINE)

The higher medical universities in Russia/Kyrgyzstan/Ukraine occupy leading positions in the world.
The world's leading medical scientists enter the various lecture halls and the wards of clinics to give their knowledge and
vast practical experience to more than 1,70,000 students inclusive of foreigners from more than 180 countries of the world, including India, who study in various medical universities every year.

PG MEDICINE

The PG course gives you an opportunity to go for the specialized understanding of your chosen specialized area or your profession.
It also helps you gain higher level of skills required in the field of medicine. The medical graduates completing their MBBS course successfully from the different MCI approved medical universities or colleges have a rewarding career ahead.
Indian medical graduates are free to go for the medical PG in China, medical PG in Russia, medical PG in Ukraine, medical PF in Armenia and more. Each country has the different eligibility criteria, specialization areas, course duration, and process for admission to their medical post-graduate programs.
A candidate can also go for Masters in Hospital Administration which has a great scope in the rapidly developing medical sector along with the varied others specializations.

PG DENTAL

Master of Dental Surgery is a postgraduate degree in Dentistry. The duration of the Master of Dental Surgery is a three- years but there is a provision
of a two-years MDS course for the candidates who have completed a Post Graduate Diploma course recognised by DCI.

AVIATION COURSE

Aviation is a broad term that covers many field areas of the industry. In a simpler term, studying aviation means you acquire technical knowledge and hands-on experience to fly or operate an aircraft. However, aviation is not merely limited to air travel. It also includes other important areas that are involved such as managing the operation that goes around flying planes.

MBA COURSE
